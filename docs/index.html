MARKS.

Audio braking markers for SimHub - a personal co-driver that learns from your fastest lap.

Download for Windows Requires SimHub · all releases

Works with iRacing, Le Mans Ultimate, Assetto Corsa, AC EVO, ACC and every other sim SimHub supports.

What it does

Learns your lap

Drive laps in learning mode; every new fastest valid lap refreshes the braking, lift and throttle markers automatically. Your fine-tuning is kept, locked markers never move.

Counts you in

Ticks lead into every marker with the final tone landing on the point itself - calibrated to your reaction time by braking along with a series of pings.

On-screen overlay

A bundled dash shows upcoming markers sliding toward a strike line, with a brake bar showing the exact pressure to match.

Yours to tune

Enable, lock, rename or nudge every marker in meters; pick per-type sounds or your own audio files.

Get started

  1. Download and run the installer - it fetches the latest build and finds your SimHub folder automatically.
  2. Start SimHub and enable the Marks plugin when prompted.
  3. Drive laps with Learning on, then switch on Play cues and follow the tones.

Advanced: tune from your desk

Marks works with SimHub's data record / replay feature. Record a session while you drive, then replay it later with no sim running: learning picks up your fastest recorded lap to build markers, and cues fire exactly as they would on track, so you can audition sounds, count-in, lead time and marker tweaks from your desk. One caveat: during a replay the brake channel comes from the recording, so use the on-screen TAP button rather than the pedal if you calibrate reaction time while replaying.

Coming soon

Stint adjustments

Markers that migrate through a stint: earlier with tyre wear, later as fuel burns off, extra-early on cold tyres after a stop.

Fuel saving

One toggle switches the whole lap into lift-and-coast: a lift cue before each braking zone, brake markers trimmed later to claw the time back.
